Owner

Mr.John Cena

John Felix Anthony Cena was born on April 23, 1977 in West Newbury, Massachusetts to Carol Cena and John Joseph Cena. He is of Italian (father) and French-Canadian and English (mother) descent, and is the grandson of baseball player Tony Lupien. When he was in college, he played football. He then continued on to be a bodybuilder and a limousine driver. The 6-foot-1 tall star weighs exactly at 240 pounds and is a very successful superstar in the WWE.

History of Club

Established in 2005 by renowned personality John Cena, Peak Performance Gym has been a cornerstone of fitness in Delhi City. Cena, a former athlete, envisioned a space where anyone could achieve their fitness goals through expert guidance and cutting-edge equipment, fostering a community of strength and vitality.

Aim behind establishment

The aim behind establishing our gym was to create a welcoming space where individuals of all fitness levels could pursue their health goals with confidence. We strive to provide expert guidance, top-notch facilities, and a supportive community to promote physical well-being and personal growth.
